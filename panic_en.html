How does it work?

The application displays schedules for a period of two hours. If there's no travel at the date, a message will invite you to select a different period.

You can select a different period, with either the clock button or by using a double-tap on the left-most (respectivly right-most) column.

Regarding the favorites, the arrow indicates the general direction (West, East,...) of the corresponding travel. Only the 5 most consulted favorites are displayed on the home page.
